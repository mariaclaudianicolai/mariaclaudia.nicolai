About Me

I am an Italian pharmacist with knowledge in both chemistry and biology. I built my pharmaceutical background by studying at the University of Salerno. After the curricular internship in the pharmacy, I started working on my experimental thesis. I focused on the identification of a novel antimicrobial peptide activity model using machine learning algorithms. Meanwhile, I collaborated with Delft University of Technology for a research project. Finally, to express the passion for technology, I co-founded the LPSystems B.V., a company specialized in designing embedded devices.
